Peach Ginger Iced Green Tea

    Ingredients:

  • -Water 400ml
  • -Lipton Peach Cinnamon Green Tea bags 3
  • -Peach deseeded & cubes 2 medium
  • -Adrak (Ginger) chopped 1 tbs
  • -Water 2 & Â½ Cups
  • -Lemon juice 1 tbs
  • -Darchini (Cinnamon stick) 1 large
  • -Honey 2-3 tbs or to taste
  • -Ice cubes
  • -Peach slices
  • -Podina (Mint leaves)

    Directions:

  1. -In a kettle,add water & bring it to boil.
  2. -Turn off the flame, add green tea bags &let it steep for 4-5 minutes.
  3. -Let it cool & refrigerate.
  4. -In a saucepan,add peach,ginger,water,lemon juice,cinnamon stick &
  5. bring it to boil,cover &cook on medium low flame for 12-15 minutes.
  6. -Turn off the flame,remove &
    7. discard cinnamon stick then blend well with the help of blender.
  7. -Add honey,mix well & set aside.
  8. -In a serving glass,add ice cubes,peach slices,prepared green tea (3/4 cup), prepared peach mixture (1/4 cup),mint leaves & stir.
  9. -Garnish with mint leaves & serve!
